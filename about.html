About Our Zoo

Welcome to the Modern Zoo, where we are dedicated to the conservation and care of animals. Our mission is to educate the public about wildlife and provide a safe haven for endangered species.

Our zoo was established in 2020 and has since become a leading institution in animal welfare and education. We are home to over 500 animals, including some of the rarest species in the world.
